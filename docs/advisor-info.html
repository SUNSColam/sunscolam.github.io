Delegate and Advisor information

Dress Code

Men

  • Must wear a suit or jacket and dress trousers (no jeans), with a dress suit and tie.
  • Dress shoes must be worn. No hats or caps will be allowed.

Women

  • Must wear formal pants and blouse or sweater. No skirts nor dresses will be allowed.
  • Make-up must be discrete.
  • Dress shoes must be worn.
  • Please avoid provocative dressing.

Accomodations

Housing

We are glad to inform that we will be offering housing, for schools that request it.

Please contact suns@colam.edu.mx with the amount of delegates needing housing. We are glad to set this up for you.
